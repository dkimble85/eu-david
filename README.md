# EU David

Scan food barcodes to instantly check if products comply with EU additive regulations.

## What is EU David?

EU David helps you make informed food choices while shopping in Europe. Simply scan any product barcode to see which additives are banned, restricted, or approved under EU food safety regulations.

The European Union maintains strict rules on food additives through EC Regulation No 1333/2008. Many additives banned in the EU are still allowed in the US, UK, and other countries. EU David bridges that information gap—so you can see exactly what's in your food.

## Features

- **📷 Barcode Scanning** — Point your camera at any food product barcode to look it up instantly
- **🔍 Additive Analysis** — See E-numbers and ingredient names categorized as:
  - **Banned** — Not permitted in EU food
  - **Restricted** — Allowed only in certain products
  - **Warning** — Requires specific labeling (e.g., may affect children)
  - **Approved** — Permitted for use
  - **Unknown** — Not in our database
- **📋 Ingredient Breakdown** — Full list of flagged ingredients with health notes and restrictions
- **📜 Scan History** — Keep track of products you've scanned (sign up to save)
- **🌍 Works Offline** — EU additive database works without internet

## How to Use

1. Tap **Start Scanning** and point your camera at a food product barcode
2. View the results—flagged ingredients are color-coded by status
3. Tap any ingredient for detailed notes and restrictions
4. Check your **History** tab for previously scanned products

## Data Source

Our additive database is based on **EC Regulation No 1333/2008** on food additives, the official EU regulation that establishes the positive list of permitted additives.

The EU operates on a "positive list" system—additives NOT on this list are banned by default. We regularly update our database as the EU Commission amends regulations.
